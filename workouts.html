Full Body Workout

2-3 days per week

  • 1. Squats - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 2. Deadlifts - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 3. Bench Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 4. Pull-ups - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 5. Shoulder Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 6. Barbell Rows - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 7. Leg Press - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 8. Bicep Curls - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 9. Tricep Dips - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Upper Body Workout

Combine this with the Lower Body Workout for 4 days per week

  • 1. Bench Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 2. Pull-ups - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 3. Shoulder Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 4. Barbell Rows - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 5. Bicep Curls - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 6. Tricep Dips - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Lower Body Workout

  • 1. Squats - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 2. Deadlifts - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • 3. Lunges - 3 sets of 8-10 reps (each leg)
  • 4. Leg Press - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 5. Leg Curl - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 6. Calf Raise - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
